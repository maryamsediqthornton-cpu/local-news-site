
# About Witney Wire Ltd

Witney Wire Ltd is an independent local news platform serving Witney and the surrounding Oxfordshire area. Our mission is to provide trusted local journalism, community updates, public-interest reporting and independent local stories.

---

# Editorial Standards

We aim to publish accurate, fair and responsible reporting. Submitted stories may be edited for clarity, grammar, legal compliance and formatting prior to publication.

Opinions expressed by contributors do not necessarily reflect the views of Witney Wire Ltd.

---

# Privacy Policy

Witney Wire Ltd respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

## Information We May Collect

- Contact details submitted through website forms
- Newsletter subscription details
- Advertising enquiry information
- Website analytics and usage information
- Voluntary story submissions and attachments

## How We Use Your Information

We use submitted information to:
- respond to enquiries
- process advertising requests
- manage newsletter subscriptions
- review editorial submissions
- improve website performance and user experience

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

## Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you may request:
- access to your data
- correction of inaccurate data
- deletion of your personal data
- restriction of processing
- withdrawal of consent where applicable

To make a request, contact:
info@witneywire.co.uk
