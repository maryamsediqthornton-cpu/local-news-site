About Witney Wire Ltd

Witney Wire Ltd is an independent local news platform serving Witney and the surrounding Oxfordshire area. Our mission is to provide trusted local journalism, community updates and independent reporting.

Editorial Standards

We aim to publish accurate, fair and responsible reporting. Submitted stories may be edited for clarity, grammar, formatting or legal compliance before publication.

Privacy Policy (UK GDPR)

Witney Wire Ltd respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal data in accordance with the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

  • We may collect contact details submitted through forms.
  • We may collect newsletter subscriptions and advertising enquiries.
  • We may use analytics tools to improve website performance.
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties.

To request access, deletion or correction of your data, email: info@witneywire.co.uk
