About Witney Wire

Witney Wire is an independent local news platform focused on community reporting, local events, public interest journalism, business coverage, and stories affecting people across Witney and the surrounding areas.

Our aim is to provide accessible, fair, and community-focused reporting that reflects the people, organisations, and issues shaping local life.

We Cover

  • Local news
  • Community events
  • Business and development
  • Public services
  • Schools and education
  • Local sport
  • Culture and entertainment
  • Opinion and analysis
Editorial decisions remain independent from advertisers and commercial partners.

Witney Wire may generate revenue through advertising, sponsorships, partnerships, and future membership initiatives to support independent local journalism.
