Community First Reporting

Witney Wire is an independent local publication focused on news, community stories, public interest journalism, business coverage, events, and the issues shaping everyday life across Witney.

Editorial decisions remain independent from advertisers, sponsors, and commercial partnerships.

Our Coverage

  • Local news and investigations
  • Business and development
  • Culture and entertainment
  • Education and public services
  • Community events
  • Sport and opinion

Supporting Independent Journalism

Witney Wire may generate revenue through advertising, sponsorships, and future membership initiatives designed to support sustainable independent local journalism.
