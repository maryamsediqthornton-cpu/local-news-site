Who We Are

Witney Wire is an independent local news platform focused on community reporting, local events, business coverage, public interest journalism, and stories affecting people across Witney and the surrounding areas.

Our Mission

Our aim is to provide accessible, fair, and community-focused reporting that reflects the people, organisations, and issues shaping local life.

Editorial Independence

Editorial decisions remain independent from advertisers, sponsors, and commercial partners.

Revenue & Support

Witney Wire may generate revenue through advertising, sponsorships, partnerships, and future membership initiatives to support independent local journalism.
