Witney swelters as temperatures hit 34°C

Witney Wire | Witney

High temperatures in Witney town centre as the town bakes in soaring sunshine. AI image for illustration purposes only.

Temperatures across Witney have climbed to 34°C this afternoon as intense sunshine grips the town centre.

Despite the unusually hot conditions, the Met Office has not issued any weather warnings for Oxfordshire.

Residents are being advised to drink plenty of water, avoid prolonged sun exposure, and check in on vulnerable neighbours, relatives, and friends during the hottest part of the day.
