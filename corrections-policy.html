Corrections Policy

Witney Wire aims to publish accurate, fair, and clearly presented local journalism. If something is wrong, unclear, or missing important context, we want to hear about it.

How To Request A Correction

Please email contact@witneywire.co.uk and include as much detail as possible so we can review the issue properly.

  • The article headline or page URL
  • The information you believe is inaccurate or incomplete
  • Any supporting evidence, source material, or clarification
  • Your name and contact details in case we need to follow up
Correction requests should be sent to:
contact@witneywire.co.uk

How We Review Corrections

We will review correction requests carefully and, where appropriate, check the original reporting, available evidence, and any relevant source material.

Depending on the issue, we may update the article, clarify wording, add a correction note, remove inaccurate information, or publish a formal correction.

Small Updates

Minor spelling, grammar, formatting, or style changes may be made without a formal correction notice where they do not alter the meaning of the article.

Our Commitment

Where a significant factual error has been made, Witney Wire will aim to correct it promptly and transparently.
