Witney Wire article workflow

Current site behaviour:
1. The homepage Top Stories section is controlled by data/articles.json.
2. app.js loads data/articles.json and sorts articles by date, newest first.
3. The first/newest article becomes the big Top Stories lead story.
4. The next four articles become the Latest list.
5. The rest appear under More from Witney Wire.
6. Category pages filter by the category field:
   - news
   - sport
   - whats-on
   - traffic
   - community
   - business

To replace the lead Top Story:
- Add or update an article in data/articles.json.
- Give it the newest date/time.
- Use category: "news" unless it belongs elsewhere.
- Put the image inside assets/.
- Set image to assets/your-image-name.png.

Important:
- The file app.js reads is data/articles.json, not the root articles.json.
- This package updates both data/articles.json and root articles.json to avoid confusion.
