Editorial Policy

Our Standards

Witney Wire is committed to fair, accurate, and responsible journalism.

  • Verify information before publication
  • Clearly distinguish news from opinion
  • Correct significant factual errors promptly
  • Provide balanced reporting where possible
  • Respect privacy and public interest considerations

Sources & Verification

Reporting may include original journalism, public records, interviews, community submissions, official statements, and press releases.

AI-Assisted Workflows

Some drafting, formatting, research, or editorial workflows may involve AI-assisted tools. Published content is reviewed before publication.

Community Standards

Witney Wire reserves the right to edit, reject, or remove submissions that are unlawful, abusive, misleading, or expose the publication to legal risk.
