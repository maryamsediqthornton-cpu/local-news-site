Editorial Policy

Witney Wire is committed to fair, accurate, and responsible journalism.

We Aim To

  • Verify information before publication
  • Clearly distinguish news from opinion
  • Correct significant factual errors promptly
  • Provide balanced reporting wherever possible
  • Respect privacy and public interest considerations
  • Avoid misleading headlines or manipulated content

Sources & Verification

Our reporting may include original reporting, public records, interviews, press releases, community submissions, and official statements.

Where information cannot be independently verified, we will make this clear within the article.

AI-Assisted Content

Some editorial workflows, formatting assistance, or research processes may involve AI-assisted tools. All published content is reviewed before publication.
