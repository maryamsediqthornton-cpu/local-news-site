Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Witney Wire respects your privacy and aims to handle personal data in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who to contact

For privacy, data protection, company or general enquiries, email info@witneywire.co.uk.

What we may collect

We may collect names, email addresses, messages, story submissions, advertising enquiries, newsletter subscription requests, and basic website analytics information.

How we use it

We use this information to respond to enquiries, manage subscriptions, consider story submissions, handle advertising enquiries, improve the website, and operate Witney Wire.

Email routing

Stories: contact@witneywire.co.uk. General/privacy: info@witneywire.co.uk. Advertising: advertise@witneywire.co.uk. Subscriptions/newsletters: subscribe@witneywire.co.uk.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data by contacting info@witneywire.co.uk.
