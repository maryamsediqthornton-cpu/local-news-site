Corrections Policy

Witney Wire is committed to correcting significant factual errors transparently and promptly.

Requesting a Correction

If you believe an article contains inaccurate information, please contact: contact@witneywire.co.uk

How Corrections Are Handled

  • Article updates
  • Clarifications
  • Correction notes
  • Removal of inaccurate material

Minor spelling or formatting changes may be made without a formal correction notice.
